Warm storybook cartoon illustration with heavy dark ink outlines and soft

the window. Slightly exaggerated cartoon proportions, hand-drawn charm, gentle

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: mobile-first loading vignette for the Swan's Office portfolio

Primary request: Create one quiet, self-contained portrait vignette of the
closed OFFICE DOOR just outside Swan's office, with exactly one large blank
pinned note that will later receive accessible DOM-rendered “come in” text. The
artwork itself must contain no words or symbols.

Input images: Image 1 is the exact Swan's Office walnut, brass, warm-plaster,
paper, ink-weight, late-afternoon lighting, gentle wear, and welcoming mood
authority. Images 2 and 3 lock the project's illustration language and color
discipline only. Do not include Swan, any duck, any character, or any atlas/grid
element.

Scene and subject: A single complete old-fashioned dark-walnut office door
centered inside a simple warm plaster doorway, viewed perfectly straight-on at
visitor eye level. The door has four restrained recessed wooden panels, visible
hand-painted walnut grain, softly worn edges, a modest aged-brass round handle
on the viewer's right, and a narrow matching walnut jamb. Show only a slim strip
of dark warm wooden floor and one simple baseboard at the bottom. A very narrow
warm amber light leak beneath the door, with the faintest green-gold hint from
the office beyond, makes the room feel occupied and welcoming without
illuminating the whole corridor.

Blank note: Pin exactly one horizontal warm-cream aged-paper note to the
upper-middle of the door at natural eye level using one tiny round aged-brass
tack centered along its top edge. The note is almost front-facing, with softly
worn paper corners, subtle fibers, a warm ink outline, and a broad calm evenly
lit blank face. It should measure about 34% of the door width and 14% of the
door height, large enough for later DOM text. Keep its central area flat and
uninterrupted. No other paper or sign anywhere.

Composition/framing: Full-bleed portrait 4:5 vignette. Keep the entire door,
jamb, handle, note, threshold, and a generous band of surrounding plaster
visible inside the central 80% of the image so a deterministic 4:5 crop cannot
cut them. Fixed camera, no tilt, no dramatic perspective, no fisheye, no open
door, no view inside the office. The door remains the sole focal object and the
note the clear secondary focal point. Quiet organized simplicity, not clutter.

Lighting and palette: warm late-afternoon corridor light; dark walnut; muted
aged brass; warm cream note; near-black warm ink, never pure black; tiny
restrained amber/green-gold line beneath the door. Soft painted shading only,
no hard cast shadows, no glossy digital gradients, no dramatic spotlight, no
horror mood, no cold grade.

Constraints: The note and every surface are completely blank. Absolutely no
“come in” lettering, text, letters, numbers, room number, nameplate, label,
office title, logo, icon, arrow, symbol, peephole, window, glass, mail slot, key,
keyhole emblem, wreath, poster, sticker, additional note, plaque, signage,
pseudo-writing, scribbles, decorative glyphs, watermark, loading spinner,
progress bar, percentage, button, control, standard-view link, or UI. No duck,
character, person, silhouette, hand, shadow figure, furniture, plant, lamp,
trophy, books, extra doorway, or visible room interior. Do not make the door
modern, ornate, gothic, photorealistic, flat-vector, glossy 3D, or ominous.

Output intent: a polished original storybook loading illustration with no
baked-in content, suitable for a fast centered mobile loader and a later DOM
overlay precisely aligned to the blank note.
